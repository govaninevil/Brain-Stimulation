Introduction

Brain stimulation has been gaining importance in the past few decades as a potent treatment option for patients suffering from a variety of mental disorders. Newer neurostimulation techniques are more focal and come with an improved safety profile as compared to the older treatment options.

Although these techniques have been used for nearly 2 decades now, the relative awareness of these amongst psychiatrists is limited. In view of expanding the scope and horizons of knowledge of psychiatrists, we are proud to organise the “Update on Non-Invasive Brain Stimulation” CME. This CME will have Update and Demonstrations for rTMS, tDCS and ECT.
